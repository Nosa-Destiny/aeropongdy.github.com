Investment & Growth Strategy

Our Path to Global Expansion

As four college engineering students with big dreams, we've developed a strategic investment approach to scale AEROpong from a classroom project to a global brand.

Bootstrap Phase

  • $15,000 personal investment from team savings (already committed)
  • University grants for student innovation projects ($5,000 secured)
  • Pre-sales revenue from early adopters (projected $20,000 in first 6 months)
  • Lean operations with all team members taking equity instead of salaries

Strategic Loans

  • $50,000 SBA microloan for equipment and inventory
  • Peer-to-peer lending through platforms like LendingClub ($25,000 target)
  • Credit line secured by initial sales ($15,000 revolving credit)
  • All loans structured with 3-5 year repayment terms

Angel Investment

  • Seeking $200,000 in exchange for 15% equity
  • Targeting sports tech investors and former athletes
  • Funds allocated to: R&D (40%), Marketing (30%), Operations (20%), Reserve (10%)
  • 3-year plan to 10x valuation through strategic partnerships

Scaling Globally

  • Series A funding target of $2M in Year 3
  • Strategic partnerships with table tennis federations
  • International distribution through sports equipment channels
  • Expand product line to include smart training systems
