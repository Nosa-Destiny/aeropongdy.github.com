About AEROpong Dynamics

Our Mission

At AEROpong Dynamics, we merge military-grade engineering with recreational innovation. Our mission is to revolutionize table tennis technology through precision engineering and hopefully integrate advanced robotics in the future.

Innovation Focus

Our team of engineers Kaesin.s, Hector.d, Cristofer & Destiny from different disciplines—mechanical, chemical, and civil engineering—are working together to combine these fields with sports science to create the most advanced table tennis training system ever developed.

Right to repair

At AEROpong Dynamics, we believe you should never be without access to your essential gear. That’s why we’re making all of our smaller assembly components available directly on our website. These files will be completely free to download for anyone who owns the AEROpong+ or the Club Edition kits. Our goal is to ensure that engineers, hobbyists, and students alike can easily reprint, replace, or iterate on the components they need—without any hassle. This initiative supports modularity, promotes sustainability, and empowers our community to innovate with freedom and confidence.

3D Printer Access

Finding affordable and reliable 3D printing services was a major hurdle. Many commercial services were too expensive for our budget, while school printers had long queues.

  • University maker spaces with student discounts
  • Local maker communities with shared resources

Rotation Mechanism Design

Creating a consistent and reliable rotation system for the ping-pong launcher proved technically challenging. We needed precise control over spin while maintaining acuracy.

  • Motor torque requirements for different spin types
  • Gear ratio calculations for optimal RPM
  • Friction management between the base and rotation system
  • Consistency across thousands of simulated repetitions
Solution: Prototyped multiple designs and settled on a more prmising design
Structural Integrity

Balancing lightweight design with structural stability was difficult, especially for the barrel assembly that needs to withstand repeated impacts.

  • Vibration during operation
  • Material fatigue over time
  • Heat dissipation from tensioned springs
  • Portability requirements
Solution: Implemented a modular strong 3D-printer filament for optimal strength-to-weight ratio
Precision Engineering
